About Gadwata

Gadwata celebrates the glorious history of Maharashtra—its magnificent forts, brave warriors, and rich cultural heritage. This project aims to showcase the pride and identity of our land.

Famous Forts

Raigad Fort

Capital fort of the Maratha Empire built by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.

Torna Fort

First fort captured by Shivaji Maharaj at age 16.

Sinhagad Fort

Famous for Tanaji Malusare’s historic valor in the Battle of Sinhagad.

Culture & Heritage

Maharashtra has a rich cultural identity—festivals, folk dance, traditional music, and the valour of Maratha warriors. Gadwata honors this glorious legacy.
